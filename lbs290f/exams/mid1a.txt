4.  This program will read an un-limited number of lines until end of file
    is reached.  Each line will contain a single integer.  At the end of the
    program the total of all of the numbers, count of the numbers, and the
    average of the numbers is to be printed.  In addition the largest number
    which was read should be printed.

    Example output:
  
    $ a.out
    Enter
    2
    Enter
    6
    Enter
    3
    Enter
    4
    Enter
    (CTRL-D)
    Largest number was 6
    Total was 15
    Count was 4
    Average was 3.7
